Retrospective - The Bullseye Activity

25 Sept 2018, by: Bhavya Shah

When there is a large project or activity to do there are usually many ideas that people have. In this activity we drew a large bullseye on the whiteboard and took turns placing yellow post it notes with our ideas written on them onto the bullseye. This was a silent activity and thus it allowed introverted members of the team to also get their ideas across. For our project we noticed that there are many ideas that we have since our project did not originate with clear guidelines. It is for these reasons that I am grateful that we did this activity in class. One thing that I love about our class is that we are learning management tools in a practical setting. Instead of just memorizing models and theories we are becoming better managers and leaders by applying our knowledge. This is why I look forward to what we will learn moving forward.
